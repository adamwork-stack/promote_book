About the Book

In his book Dream Big, Bob Goff describes how he observed sea otters floating in the water, arm in arm. Always in pairs. They hold on to take care of each other. They don't want any otter in their group to get lost.

Preparing for this book, I spent a few days with Bob, and he impacted me in a big way. Life Punch is a collection of real-life stories. Some short, some long. Some serious, some silly. Each one reminds us that we should hold on to God and each other.

When life hits hard, we need community. We need faith. We need to hold on—together.

Real Stories. Real Faith. Real Community.

Stories that inspire, challenge, and remind you that you're not alone.

About the Author

Doug James Hood

Doug is on a mission to build and preserve community. Through his writing, speaking, and daily life, he encourages others to hold on to God and each other—especially when life hits hard.

His stories are drawn from real experiences: moments of grace, moments of struggle, and the powerful truth that we're better together. Doug believes in the power of connection, the strength of faith, and the importance of showing up for one another.
